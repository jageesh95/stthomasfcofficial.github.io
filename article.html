Leauge Details

The Leauge is contected in pes mobile game. The main idea of pes football, is we can create our own team with our favourite players, Like wise we also designd st thomas leauge.

  • Each team will have a total of 300M in their Account
  • Each team can buy upto 25 players and minimum 18 players
  • Auction will be held to buy players
  • Players can be swapped only during mid or end of season
  • Total 11 teams in this league
  • All team will play against each other, so there will be a total of 10 league matches
  • Top 4 team will qualify for next round
  • 1st and 2nd place will play Qualifier round
  • 3rd and 4th place will play Eliminator
  • Winner will be awarded with 40M price money
  • Base and futured players will play the tournament
  • No Legend or iconic player is allowed in this tournament
  • Match will be played for 8 mins.

About Auction

The auction was conducted by the organizer Jinu. Initially all teams will be given the purse balance of 300 million. A total of 300 players have registered for the STL Auctions. New players can be added to the auctions if there is requests from franchise. Every franchise should buy minimum of 18 to maximum of 25 players.
