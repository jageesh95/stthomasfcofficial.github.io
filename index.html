About football

Football (literally "ball to the foot" in English),commonly called football,is a sport that is practiced with 22 players or 16 players for people under the age of 14 (a referee on the field and two referees of touch, as well as two other surface referees in some competitions) with a leather round ball or something else: two teams of 11 players compete and must put the ball in the goal of the opposing camp without using the hands (except the goalkeeper).

The league currently comprises 11 clubs.

During the league stage of the competition, each club plays against all the other clubs in a round-robin style. At the end of the league stage the team with the most points gets declared the Premiers and presented with a trophy named League Winners' Shield, and the top four clubs qualifies for the play-offs.
